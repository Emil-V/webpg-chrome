WebPG for Chrome/Chromium.

webpg-chrome is a browser extension that provides GnuPG related Public/Private key operations in Google Chrome/Chromium. With webpg-chrome you can detect inline PGP Blocks, Sign, Encrypt and perform Decryption. webpg-chrome also provides a full-featured Key Manager that allows for creation and management of Public and Private Keys. You can manage trust assignment, signatures and revocation.

WebPG is free

WebPG is completely of charge, free of ads, and free malware. It was created with the goal of making GnuPG management and operation easy and user friendly.

What do I need?

In order to use WebPG, you will need a working version of GnuPG installed, as well as a key-agent.
